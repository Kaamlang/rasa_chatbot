Admission Procedure

Students who have completed their higher secondary final exams can directly apply to the college with their academic certificates,

citizenship certificate or passport or birth certificate or citizenship of either of the parents. Applicants should be minimum 18 years of age and

should possess the minimum qualifications mentioned here under.

Bachelor's in Computer Science

  • Academic Level
    • Completion of NEB +2 Qualification with an aggregate ≥ 60% or 2.2 CGPA*
    • 2 ‘A’ level passes with minimum grades A to C or 3 ‘A’ level subjects with minimum grades D and above.
  • English
    • English NEB XII Marks ≥ 60% or 2.4 GPA*
    • Pass in GP or English in A or AS Levels
  • Maths
    • Maths NEB XII Marks ≥ 60% or 2.4 GPA
    • Pass in GP or Maths in A or AS Levels
